Chocolate Chip cookies

prepration time :15 mins

cooking time :10 mins

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350oF(180oC).
  2. Mix flour and sugar in a bowl
  3. Add chocolate chips and stir well
  4. Bake for 10 minutes or until golden brown.

Ingredients

ingredients Quantity
flour 2 cups
sugar 1 cup
chocolates chips 1 cup
